Projects

Insight

This is my team's project from the course COGS 120/CSE 170. It is a webapp with an intended audience of students who are taking online class, specifically in the context of asynchronous learning. It aims to keep students engaged with their lectures and organized with their tasks, using various features such as a to-do list and the ability to mark certain timestamps of their lecture videos with notes.

Quizlet Jr.

This is mobile app project that I worked on in order to become familiar with Android Studio. It's a simple flashcard app, with the ability to create new flashcards, tap the current card to reveal the answer, edit the current card, and switch between cards.
